About Us

AbAMPdb is an open repository to screen potential antimicrobial peptides against Acinetobacter baumannii. Our current index is version 1 and searches more than 150 publications to screen 30 Potential AMPs from different resources. All the data based on AMPs and their interaction with A. baumannii Omp38 protein Uniprot ID is Q6RYW5 freely available through search index and can be downloaded via available formats.
